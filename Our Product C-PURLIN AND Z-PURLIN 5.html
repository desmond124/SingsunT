Sing Sun produces a range of galvanized C & Z-Purlins and girts for building construction applications. Manufactured from quality galvanized steel, are available either plain or with textured surfaces on the web. Pre-punched holes either round or elongated on the webs are custom drilled according to requirements. All galvanized purlins are manufactured from high tensile steel fro increased performance and efficiency, reduced weight, and durability.
